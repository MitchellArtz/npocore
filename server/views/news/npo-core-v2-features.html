What's new in NPO Core v2.0?

We wanted to make NPO Core more flexible and beneficial to registered NPOs, so we decided to make some major upgrades!

Here are some of the new features we've implemented in NPO Core v2.0!

  • Official accounts for registered NPOs
    • Register and update the information on your NPO profile
    • Better branding and more information for each NPO
    • Add your logo
    • Add all your social media links
  • Official 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization verification!
    • Submit a form for us to verify your NPO
    • If it is official you will receive:
      • A special badge on your organization
      • A huge NPO Score boost!
  • Opportunity postings
    • Post and promote any organization updates or events!
    • Users can now subscribe to your organization!
    • Every time you post an opportunity, they will get an email notification!
  • NPO Score
    • More on NPO Scores here!
  • Geographical organization map
    • Submit your NPO's location
    • Be placed on a geographical map
    • Allow users to find your NPO by location!
  • Featured organizations/opportunity posts!
    • We feature 3 organizations/posts every week!
    • Gives featured NPOs/opportunity posts:
      • More reach
      • More advertising/screentime
      • How do you get featured?
        • Be active on NPO Core
        • Refer other organizations to join on NPO Core
        • Brand your profile well!
  • More accurate organization filtering + searching
